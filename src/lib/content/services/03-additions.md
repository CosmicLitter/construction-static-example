Additions are half construction, half surgery. The trick is making the new
part feel like it always belonged — matching rooflines, siding, trim, and
interior details so no one can tell where old meets new.

Whether it's a primary suite, a second story, or a mudroom that finally
solves the boot situation, we plan carefully so you can keep living in your
home through the build.
