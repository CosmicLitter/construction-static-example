Ironwood Construction was founded in 2008 by Tom Caldwell, a third-generation
carpenter who grew up swinging a hammer on his grandfather's job sites in
western Montana. After two decades of framing custom homes for other builders,
Tom set out to start a company where the people doing the work actually owned
the work.

Today Ironwood is a small, tight-knit team of craftspeople who treat every
project like it's going up next door — because often, it is. We build homes,
renovations, and commercial spaces across the Gallatin Valley, and our clients
find us almost entirely through word of mouth.

We believe a well-built home should outlast the people who live in it. That
means honest materials, careful details, and a crew that shows up on time,
cleans up after itself, and stands behind its work long after the final
walkthrough.
