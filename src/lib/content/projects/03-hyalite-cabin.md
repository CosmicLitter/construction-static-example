We helped the owners right-size their retirement plans: 1,600 square feet,
one thoughtful bedroom, a screened porch for long summer evenings, and a
great room sized for the grandkids when they visit. The cabin is tucked
among existing trees with minimal site disturbance — you don't see it from
the water until you're almost at the dock.
