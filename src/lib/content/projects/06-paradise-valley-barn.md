The barn was worth saving — hand-hewn beams, square-nail construction,
exactly the kind of bones you can't reproduce. We reinforced the frame,
infilled with SIP panels for a tight envelope, and carved out a two-bedroom
guest house below a north-lit painting studio on the upper floor.

Nothing about it looks new, and that was the point.
