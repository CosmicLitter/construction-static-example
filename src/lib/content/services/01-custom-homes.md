We build roughly four to six custom homes a year. Each one starts with a
conversation about the land, the light, and the way your family moves through
a day. We work closely with your architect — or help you find one — and stay
on the job from site prep through the final coat of wax on the floors.

Our specialty is durable, timeless houses that hold up to Montana weather and
the next forty years of life.
