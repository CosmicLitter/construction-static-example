The owners wanted to host without being stranded on the wrong side of a
wall. We took out the load-bearing partition, added a steel beam, and
reworked the footprint around a generous island. Custom white oak cabinets
run to the ceiling, a soapstone perimeter handles the daily wear, and a La
Cornue range anchors the room.

Three months on the nose, start to finish — exactly what we committed to at
contract.
